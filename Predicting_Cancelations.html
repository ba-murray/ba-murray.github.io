Predicting Hotel Reservations Cancellations¶

Introduction¶

During the pandemic hotels were forced to add full refund policies for reservations Three years later, the trend still stands. The experience of trying to cancel any type of travel arrangement in 2020 has had a huge impact on consumer behavior. Today, many people will not book unless there is a free cancelation button. As a result of the free cancellation policies, rebooking activity has increased with canceling of existing reservations and booking of the same rooms as rates are slashed to secure occupancy. Hotels with low occupancy rates are particularly vulnerable to the change in the consumer. Data shows that higher guest engagement and personalization may result in lower cancellation and rebooking rates

Goal¶

Hotel Delfino wants to target high-risk guests with a campaign of perks and discounts to lower cancellations and rebookings. To do so, executives need a method to predict the probability that a guest will cancel their reservation.

Initial Findings¶

  • A large majority of guests do not cancel their reservations.
  • The typical guest that has the highest probability to keep their reservation: books their travel plans under two months in advance, is a repeat guest, books during the Christmas holidays, and is in town for work.
  • The guests most likely to cancel: book well in advance, book online with a more expensive room, have never stayed at the hotel, and arrive during the summer months.

Conclusion¶

Accuracy may not be reliable due to class imbalance; however, F-1 score was strong. AUC score can be a reliable metric for model performance with skewed data. Methods such as random oversampling techniques can be used to create a balanced target variable.The Random Forest ensemble method has the highest accuracy and AUC metric for performance.

Next Steps¶

  • The most accurate method was an esemble training method, similar methods such as bagging or boosting may provide better results
  • standardization methods can be used improve overall accuracy
  • Tuning hyperparameters can also potentially yield better results through methods such as gridsearch or randomizedsearch
